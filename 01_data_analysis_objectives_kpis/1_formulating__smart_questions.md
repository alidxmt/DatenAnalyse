### Data Analysis Task: SMART Question Formulation

**Task 1: Customer Satisfaction**
* **Scenario:** A company notices that their product ratings are dropping.
* **Goal:** Formulate a SMART question to identify the root cause.

**Task 2: Marketing Effectiveness**
* **Scenario:** You want to analyze if sending more emails increases sales.
* **Goal:** Create a SMART question using an Actionable KPI rather than a vanity metric.

**Task 3: Pricing Strategy**
* **Scenario:** Management is considering a price increase for the next month.
* **Goal:** Draft a SMART question that is Prescriptive (Was sollen wir tun?).
