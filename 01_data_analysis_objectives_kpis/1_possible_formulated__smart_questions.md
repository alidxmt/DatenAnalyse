### Exercise Solutions

**Solution 1: Customer Satisfaction (Diagnostic)**
* **SMART Question:** "Did the average delivery time increase by more than 20% in the last 30 days, causing the 1-star rating frequency to double?" 
* **Why:** It is Specific to delivery times and Measurable via rating frequency.

**Solution 2: Marketing Effectiveness (Actionable KPI)**
* **SMART Question:** "Will increasing the email campaign frequency from once to twice weekly result in a 5% increase in the click-through rate over the next month?"
* **Why:** It focuses on the "Engagement rate" (Actionable) rather than just the "Number of emails sent" (Vanity).

**Solution 3: Pricing Strategy (Prescriptive)**
* **SMART Question:** "How will a 10% price increase on top-tier products affect the total sales volume and customer retention rate by the end of next month?" 
* **Why:** It is Time-bound and provides a clear recommendation for action.
